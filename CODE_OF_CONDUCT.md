# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
AegisAI platform project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward others
- Respect differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them
- Focus on what is best for the platform and its users

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexual language or imagery in any project space
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct should be reported to the Chief Enterprise
Architect at conduct@aegisai.internal. All reports will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (GitHub, Slack, meetings)
and public spaces where an individual is representing the project.
